avācyo vācakairdharmaḥ kṛpayā yena deśitaḥ|namo 'cintyaprabhavāya buddhāyāsaṅgabuddhaye||8

svabhāvena na cotpannā nirvṛtāśca na tattvataḥ|yathākāśaṃ tathā buddhāḥ sattvāścaivaikalakṣaṇāḥ||9

pārāvāraṃ na cotpannāḥ svabhāvena pratityajāḥ|te 'pi śūnyā hi saṃskārāḥ sarvajñajñānagocarāḥ||10

sarvabhāvāḥ svabhāvena pratibimbasamā matāḥ|śuddhāḥ śivasvabhāvāśca advayāstathatāsamāḥ||11

asatyātmani cātmatvaṃ kalpayitvā pṛthagjanāḥ|sukhaduḥkham abhijñāśca sarvam eṣāṃ ca tattvataḥ||12

ṣaḍgatiryaśca saṃsāraḥ svargaśca paramaṃ sukham|narake ca mahadduḥkhaṃ jarāvyādhirapī yatām||13

abhūtāṃ kalpanāṃ kṛtvā pacyante narakādiṣu|svadoṣenaiva dahyante veṇavo vahninā yathā||14

yathā māyā tathā sattvā viṣayān paribhuñjate||māyāmayīṃ gatiṃ yānti pratītyotpādarūpiṇīm||15

yathā citrakaro rūpaṃ yakṣasyātibhayaṅkaraṃ|bibheti svayam ālikhya saṃsāre 'py abudhastathā||16

yathā paṅkaṃ svayaṃ kṛtvā kaścit patati bāliśaḥ|tathāsatkalpanāpaṅke magnāḥ sattvā duruttare||17

abhāvaṃ bhavato dṛṣṭvā duḥkhāṃ vindati vedanām|śaṅkāviṣeṇa bādhante viṣayā vitathāstathā||18

tāṃscaivāśaraṇān dṛṣṭvā karuṇādhīramānasāḥ|niyojayanti saṃbodhau sattvān buddhā hitaṃkarāḥ||19

te 'pi saṃbhṛtasaṃbhārāḥ prāpya jñānam anuttaram|kalpanājālanirmuktā buddhā syurlokabandhavaḥ||20

yato 'jātam anutpannaṃ samyak sattvārthadarśinaḥ|tataḥ śūnyaṃ jagad dṛṣṭvā ādimadhyāntavarjitam||21

tena paśyanti saṃsāraṃ nirvāṇaṃ ca na cātmanaḥ|nirlepaṃ nirvikāraṃ ca ādimadhyāntabhāsvaram||22

svapnānubhūtaviṣayaṃ pratibuddho na paśyati|mohanidrāvibuddhaśca saṃsāraṃ naiva paśyati||23

māyāṃ vidhāya māyavī upasaṃharate yadā|tadā na vidyate kiṃcid dharmāṇāṃ sā hi dharmatā||24

cittamātram idaṃ sarvaṃ māyākāravad utthitam|tataḥ śubhāśubhaṃ karma tato janma śubhāśubham||25

kalpayanti yathā lokaṃ notpannāśca svayaṃ janāḥ|utpādo hi vikalpo 'yaṃ artho bāhyo na vidyate||26

asvabhāveṣu bhāveṣu nityātmasukhasaṃjñinaḥ|bhavārṇave bhramantyasmin bālā mohatamovṛtāḥ||27kalpanājalapūrṇasya
